About Us

Ship Anywhere With QuickShip

Established in 1938, QuickShip has always been at the service of its customers aiming to provide the best service with its physical presence in 3 continents and cross-border solutions.

QuickShip is engaged in shipping, energy, transportation, offshore drilling, and retail activities. It operates through the following segments: Ocean, Logistics and Services, Terminals and Towage, and Manufacturing and Others. The Logisticsco and Services segment comprises freight forwarding, supply chain management, inland haulage, and other logistics services.
